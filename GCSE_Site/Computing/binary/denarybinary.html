Denary to Binary
It might be a little harder to convert from denary to binary:
  • Decide the number of bits you need using 2^n (e.g. if your number is lower than 256, that's 2^8 which is 8 bits).
  • Put a 1 in the leftmost place and subtract its value from your denary.
  • Put a 1 in the next place lower than the number you have left.
  • Repeat steps 2 and 3 until you have 0 left.
