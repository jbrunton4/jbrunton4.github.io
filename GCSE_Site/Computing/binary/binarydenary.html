Binary to Denary
It's easy to convert a binary number to a denary number:
  • Start at the right
  • Begin with 0 as your denary number
  • Is the number a 1? If yes, add 1 to your denary number
  • Move a space to the left.
  • Is the number a 1? If yes, add 2 to your denary number
  • Move a space to the left.
  • Is the number a 1? If yes, add 4 to your denary number
  • Repeat, each time adding twice what you would have before, until you get to the end of the number.
